How We Store & Protect Your Data

All data is stored on Google Firebase (Google Cloud Platform), which provides industry-leading security infrastructure:

  • Encryption in transit: All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS 1.3
  • Encryption at rest: Your data is encrypted at rest on Firebase servers
  • Authentication: Only you can access your data through your authenticated account. Firebase Security Rules enforce user-level isolation â€” no other user can read or write your documents
  • Photos: Stored in Firebase Storage with authenticated-only access URLs
  • No plaintext passwords: Authentication is managed by Firebase Auth using industry-standard password hashing

While we implement strong safeguards, no digital system is 100% impenetrable. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password and enable device-level security (PIN, biometric lock).

Location Data

Room Logger requests access to your device's GPS for one specific purpose: to embed location coordinates into photos taken during property inspections.

  • When: GPS is only captured at the moment you take a photo inside the app. We never collect background location.
  • Why: GPS coordinates appear in your PDF report as verification that photos were taken at the property, adding legal weight to your documentation.
  • What's stored: Latitude and longitude of each photo, stored alongside that photo's Firestore record.
  • Control: You can deny location permission. The app will still function; your photos will simply have no GPS metadata embedded.

Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is yours. You can delete it at any time.

  • Active account: Your data is retained as long as your account exists and you choose to keep it.
  • Delete account: Go to Settings â†’ Delete Account. All your data â€” profile, properties, rooms, photos, and reports â€” is permanently and irreversibly deleted from our servers within 30 days.
  • Individual properties: You can delete individual properties and their associated rooms, photos, and reports at any time from the app.
  • Backup copies: Firebase may retain encrypted backups for up to 30 days for disaster recovery purposes, after which they are overwritten.

Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered. We recommend downloading any reports you wish to keep before deleting your account.

Children's Privacy

Room Logger is designed for adults who rent residential property. The app is not directed to, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from, children under the age of 13 (or under 16 in the European Economic Area).

If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@roomlogger.app and we will promptly delete it.
